Speakers

Meet our extraordinary speakers who bring unparalleled excitement and a wealth of knowledge to our tour. Their insights and expertise make every moment a learning adventure, ensuring an enriching experience for all participants.

Day 1

PAGASA Weather Station, First Aviation Academy, and Vessel Traffic Management System
Arnel G. Gonzales
PAGASA Weather Station

Mr. Arnel provided a detailed overview of PAGASA's crucial role in weather forecasting and monitoring. His explanations helped us grasp the scientific processes behind studying and predicting weather patterns.

Dave Del Rosario
FAA Sales and Marketing Staff

Mr. Dave provided us with a comprehensive demonstration of aircraft operations, including a detailed walkthrough of aircraft systems and a hands-on experience with flight simulators. His expertise shed light on the intricacies of aviation technology, enriching our understanding of how airplanes function in both theory and practice.

Jerome D. Saddi
VTMS Spokeman

His detailed explanation effectively elucidated the workings of the vessel traffic management system, complemented by a practical demonstration using navigational maps. His expertise provided invaluable insights into the operational intricacies and strategic importance of maritime traffic management, enhancing our comprehension of this critical system.

Day 2

QBO Innovation Hub and PacketWorx Inc. IoT Technologies Hub
Karla Legaspi
QBO Secretary

Ms. Karla provided a comprehensive overview of QBO Inc.'s role in nurturing startups and fostering innovation. She emphasized their commitment to empowering computer science students to leverage technology as a tool for addressing societal challenges.

Rose Ann Caraan
Packetworx Staff

She demonstrated various devices developed by Packetworx, illustrating their applications in critical areas such as flood monitoring and water metering. These IoT devices serve as essential tools in addressing diverse environmental and infrastructural challenges.

Day 3

Light Rail Transit Authority-Line 2 and National Museum of the Philippines
Adrian Saldana
LRT Line-2 Staff

His clear explanations helped us understand how safe and efficient public transportation works, giving us insights into the technology and systems at LRT 2. Mr. Saldana's passion for his work made our time at LRT 2 both educational and inspiring.

Bernadine P. Bernadino
Museum Staff

Ms. Bernadine guided us through the National Museum, presenting a collection of artworks by renowned Filipino artists. Her tour provided insightful narratives on the cultural significance and artistic contributions of these notable pieces, enriching our understanding of Filipino heritage and creativity.

Day 4

98 Labs INC and HYTEC POWER INC
Brian Tan Seng
98 Labs CEO

Mr. Brian, CEO of 98 Labs, demonstrated the pivotal role of computer science in addressing contemporary societal challenges driven by technology. His compelling life story and insights inspired us to deepen our commitment to academic excellence and innovation. His presentation underscored the transformative potential of technology, motivating us to harness our skills to make meaningful contributions to society.

Gretchel Listana
Hytec Secretary

She provided an insightful overview of HYTEC's commitment to nurturing students, particularly those interested in engineering and IT disciplines. We were introduced to their advanced simulators, innovative devices, and robotic technologies, showcasing their impactful role in preparing future professionals. Her presentation highlighted how HYTEC equips students with practical skills and knowledge essential for thriving in the fields of engineering and technology.
