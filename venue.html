DJM Domitory

24 Seminary Road, Brgy. Bahay Toro, Quezon City, Metro Manila

During our tour in Manila, we lodged at DJM Dormitory for over a week, benefiting from well-appointed, air-conditioned rooms and comprehensive amenities that ensured our comfort throughout our stay.

PAGASA Weather Station, First Aviation Academy (FAA), and Vessel Traffic Management System

Building 8050 Old Terminal, Argonaut Highway, Cubi Point, Subic Bay Freeport Zone 2222, Morong, Bataan

Packetworx Inc - IoT Technology Hub

National Museum of the Philippines

While in Manila, we toured the National Museum of the Philippines at Padre Burgos Ave, Ermita, Manila, 1000 Metro Manila, where we explored various exhibits showcasing the rich cultural and historical heritage of the Philippines.

HYTEC POWER INC.
